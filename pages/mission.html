My Mission

My mission is to provide everyone on the internet with a sense of confidence while browsing or developing, by integrating our foundational approach of open-minded development.

Here are some of the things I'm focused on:

  • Developing "BxrdNet", an online platform that revolves around the principles of free speech, free thinking, and free, unregulated information.
  • Addressing internet privacy issues and demystifying the complex aspects of the internet and online protocols.
  • Providing a platform that encourages free speech and free thinking.
  • Creating this site as an Interactive Learning Tool
