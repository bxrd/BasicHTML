About Me

Hi, I'm Tyler, also known as "Bxrd". I'm a former Software Development student from Iowa who unfortunately had to drop out due to COVID. However, that didn't stop me from pursuing my passion for technology.

Currently, I'm focused on developing "BxrdNet", an online platform that revolves around the principles of free speech, free thinking, and free, unregulated information. I'm particularly interested in addressing internet privacy issues and demystifying the complex aspects of the internet and online protocols.

My ultimate goal is to provide everyone on the internet with a sense of confidence while browsing or developing, by integrating our foundational approach of open-minded development.

Here are some of the things I enjoy:

  • Coding
  • Philosophy
  • Graphic Design
  • Playing guitar/ Writing Lyrics
  • Teaching
